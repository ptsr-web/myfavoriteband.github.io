Gojira

My favorite metal band.

  Gojira is a French progressive metal band. Founded in 1996 in Ondry near Bayonne under the name Godzilla (until 2001). Gojira has released seven studio albums, three live albums, and is the first French band to reach the top of the Billboard charts (best heavy rock album). Gojira was nominated for a Grammy in the category of Best Metal Album for Magma, as well as Best Metal Performance for the singles “Silvera” and “Amazonia”. Gojira holds the record for the loudest concert (and sound in general) at the Stade de France. The band is also known for its environmental themes and environmental activism. Gojira was the first heavy metal band to perform at the opening ceremony of the 2024 Summer Olympics in France.
